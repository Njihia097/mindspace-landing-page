FAQs

How does the guided meditation work? +

Our guided meditations are led by experienced instructors and cover various techniques including breathing exercises, body scans, and visualization practices. You can choose sessions based on your experience level and available time.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial that gives you access to all premium features. No credit card required to start your trial.

Can I use the app offline? +

Absolutely! You can download your favorite meditation sessions and wellness content to use offline, perfect for when you're traveling or in areas with limited connectivity.

What makes your approach different? +

We combine ancient wisdom with modern science, offering personalized wellness programs that adapt to your progress and preferences. Our holistic approach addresses mind, body, and spirit.

Is customer support available? +

Yes, our support team is available 24/7 through chat, email, and phone. We're here to help you get the most out of your wellness journey.
